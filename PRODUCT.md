# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small group of friends using a shared bucket list and personal life goals tracker. They open the app in spare moments — a quiet evening, a weekend morning — to log things they want to do, mark progress, and stay gently accountable to their own ambitions.

## Product Purpose

TouchGrass helps people capture, organize, and pursue bucket list items and life goals. Success looks like a user opening the app regularly because it feels good to be there, not because it demands their attention.

## Brand Personality

Minimal, calm, personal. The app feels like a well-designed notebook: quietly considered, never loud, always ready.

Voice: warm but restrained. English primary language. Short, precise labels. No marketing energy inside the product.

## Anti-references

- Brutalism (heavy borders, raw type, harsh contrast)
- Gradient-heavy UI (decorative gradient text, rainbow fills, gradient-as-identity)
- AI slop patterns: glassmorphism cards as default, identical icon-grid card layouts, eyebrows above every section, indigo SaaS defaults
- SaaS dashboard aesthetics (data-dense, metric-heavy, corporate blue palettes)

## Design Principles

1. **Quiet confidence** — the UI never shouts. Space and restraint do the work. If something needs emphasis, reduce surrounding noise rather than adding decoration.
2. **Personal, not generic** — every screen should feel like it was made for one person, not a product category. Avoid anything that reads as "todo app template."
3. **Earn every element** — nothing on screen without purpose. No decorative dividers, no filler icons, no labels that repeat the obvious.
4. **Apple-caliber materiality** — clean geometry, considered spacing, typography that leads the eye. Surfaces feel physical without being gimmicky.
5. **Quiet excitement** — the emotional register is anticipation, not urgency. Visual energy comes from composition and detail, not motion or color saturation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum across all surfaces
- Sufficient contrast for body text (≥4.5:1), large text (≥3:1)
- Full keyboard navigation and visible focus indicators
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations
- English primary language; font choice must support clean rendering at all weights
